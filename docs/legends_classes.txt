Apex Legends organises all playable characters into five classes: Assault, Skirmisher, Recon, Controller, and Support. Each class has a shared passive ability in addition to the legend's individual abilities.

Assault legends are designed for aggressive, offensive play. Their class passive allows them to open and access extra loot from red supply bins, which contain higher-tier gear not accessible to other classes. Assault legends include Ash, Bangalore, Fuse, Mad Maggie, Ballistic, and Catalyst was reclassified. Bangalore is a versatile legend known for her smoke launchers and Rolling Thunder airstrike ultimate. Fuse specialises in explosive damage with his Knuckle Cluster tactical and Motherlode ultimate that creates a ring of fire. Ash can reveal the location of recent deathboxes on the minimap using her passive and uses a phase snare as her tactical.

Skirmisher legends excel at mobility and repositioning. Their class passive allows them to preview the contents of a care package before it lands, giving them a strategic advantage when competing for high-tier loot drops. Skirmisher legends include Wraith, Pathfinder, Octane, Horizon, Valkyrie, Revenant, and Alter. Octane is one of the most popular legends and can place a Launch Pad with his ultimate that allows the entire squad to bounce to new positions. Pathfinder can grapple to high ground and create ziplines for the team. Horizon has strong vertical mobility with her Gravity Lift tactical.

Recon legends are built for information gathering and map awareness. Their class passive allows them to scan survey beacons found around the map to reveal the next Ring location, giving their squad advance knowledge for rotation planning. Recon legends include Bloodhound, Crypto, Seer, and Vantage. Bloodhound is the most played Recon legend and can track enemy movements with Eye of the Allfather and chase enemies down with their Beast of the Hunt ultimate. Seer can interrupt heals and revives with his tactical ability.

Controller legends specialise in area denial and securing zones. Their class passive allows them to access a survey of the surrounding area by interacting with a ring console inside the Ring, providing a detailed breakdown of the ring's position. Controller legends include Caustic, Wattson, Rampart, and Catalyst. Wattson is exceptionally strong in defensive final ring situations, using her Perimeter Security fences and Interception Pylon ultimate to lock down buildings. Caustic places poisonous gas traps that deal damage and slow enemies inside buildings. Rampart deploys Amped Cover barriers and the Sheila minigun ultimate.

Support legends focus on keeping teammates alive and providing utility. Their class passive allows them to craft banner cards at replicators even after the Fabrication timer has expired, giving their squad more opportunities to respawn eliminated teammates. Support legends include Lifeline, Loba, Newcastle, and Conduit. Lifeline is the primary healing legend and can revive teammates while using her hands for other actions, plus her Care Package ultimate delivers healing items and gear. Loba provides the squad with sustained ammo and supply through her Black Market Boutique ultimate. Newcastle can protect allies while reviving them by dragging them behind his shield.
