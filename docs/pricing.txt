TaskFlow offers four pricing tiers: Free, Starter, Business, and Enterprise.

The Free plan is available at no cost and supports up to 5 team members and 3 active projects. It includes unlimited tasks, basic Kanban boards, 1 GB of file storage, and access to the iOS and Android mobile apps. The Free plan does not include custom fields, time tracking, or reporting dashboards. It also excludes API access and webhook integrations.

The Starter plan costs $9 per user per month when billed annually, or $12 per user per month when billed monthly. It supports unlimited projects and up to 25 team members. Starter adds 10 GB of file storage per user, custom fields on tasks, basic time tracking, CSV export, and email support with a 48-hour response time.

The Business plan costs $22 per user per month when billed annually, or $28 per user per month when billed monthly. It includes everything in Starter plus unlimited team members, 100 GB of file storage per user, advanced reporting dashboards, portfolio views, workload management, webhook integrations, priority email support with a 24-hour response time, and single sign-on (SSO) via SAML 2.0.

The Enterprise plan offers custom pricing negotiated directly with the TaskFlow sales team. It is designed for organizations with more than 500 users or complex compliance requirements. Enterprise includes everything in Business plus dedicated account management, custom SLA agreements, on-premise deployment options, advanced audit logs, 24/7 phone and chat support, and a 99.99% uptime SLA. Organizations interested in Enterprise should contact sales@taskflow.io.

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. Annual billing provides a discount equivalent to two months free compared to monthly billing.
