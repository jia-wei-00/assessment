TaskFlow Support Policy — Effective January 1, 2024

TaskFlow provides different levels of support depending on the customer's plan.

Free and Starter plan customers receive email support only. Support requests should be submitted through the Help Center at help.taskflow.io or by emailing support@taskflow.io. The target response time for Free plan customers is 5 business days. Starter customers receive a 48-hour response time on business days (Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM Central Time, excluding US public holidays).

Business plan customers receive priority email support with a guaranteed first response within 24 hours on business days. Business customers also gain access to live chat support available Monday through Friday from 8 AM to 8 PM Central Time.

Enterprise customers receive 24/7 support via phone, live chat, and email. Each Enterprise account is assigned a dedicated Customer Success Manager who conducts quarterly business reviews and assists with onboarding, training, and escalations. Enterprise SLAs guarantee a 1-hour response time for critical (P1) issues and a 4-hour response time for high (P2) issues.

All customers have access to the TaskFlow Help Center, which contains over 400 articles, video tutorials, and a searchable community forum. The Help Center is available 24/7 without requiring a login.

TaskFlow observes the following US public holidays during which support response times may be extended: New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day.

To escalate a support issue, customers can reply to any support email with "ESCALATE" in the subject line, or ask the live chat agent to escalate to a senior support engineer. Enterprise customers can contact their Customer Success Manager directly for escalations.

Customers who believe they have found a security vulnerability should report it via the dedicated security disclosure form at security.taskflow.io rather than the general support channel. TaskFlow operates a responsible disclosure program and aims to acknowledge security reports within 24 hours.
