Apex Legends features five active maps that rotate in regular and ranked playlists: Kings Canyon, Worlds Edge, Olympus, Storm Point, and Broken Moon.

Kings Canyon is the original Apex Legends map and was available at launch in February 2019. It is a medium-sized map with diverse terrain including canyon passages, open fields, and tight indoor areas. Key points of interest include Skull Town, a popular hot drop with dense building clusters, Relay, Artillery, and the Market area. Kings Canyon is known for encouraging aggressive close-range combat due to its tight corridors and multiple flanking routes.

Worlds Edge was introduced in Season 3 and is set on a partially volcanic planet. The map features a mix of dense urban areas and open lava terrain. Fragment East and Fragment West are the most popular hot drop locations and offer intense early-game fights in a destroyed city environment. Lava Siphon, Survey Camp, and Epicentre are other notable points of interest. The Train is a moving vehicle that travels a fixed route around the map and can be looted while in motion.

Olympus was introduced in Season 7 and is a floating sky city above a gas giant planet. The map has an open layout with many wide plazas, tall buildings, and elevated walkways. Phase Runner is a teleportation tunnel that connects two opposite sides of the map, allowing rapid rotation. Estates, Bonsai Plaza, Gardens, and Grow Towers are key areas. Olympus favours long to medium-range engagements due to its open sightlines and elevated terrain.

Storm Point was introduced in Season 11 and is the largest map in Apex Legends. It is set on a tropical island with varied terrain including beaches, cliffs, jungle, and elevated ruins. The map features Spider Nests and Prowler Dens that contain AI-controlled creatures which drop loot when killed, providing an alternative source of equipment beyond regular floor loot. Key points of interest include Checkpoint, Barometer, Command Center, and Highpoint. ECHO Camps are large circular outposts that contain ziplines for traversal and offer strong defensive positions. Storm Point suits squads with strong repositioning legends due to its large size.

Broken Moon was introduced in Season 15 and is set on a terraformed moon. The map features a unique Zip Rail system, which consists of large fixed ziplines connecting major points of interest across the map, allowing rapid traversal over long distances. Key areas include Terraformer, Eternal Gardens, The Divide, Backup Atmo, and Stasis Array. The map has a mix of industrial interiors and open outdoor terrain.

Map rotation in Apex Legends changes the active map on a timer throughout each season. Ranked and regular modes often run different maps simultaneously. Players can check the active map rotation on the main menu or on third-party websites such as Apex Legends Status. Some limited-time events temporarily change map availability.

Survey Beacons are marked locations on every map used exclusively by Recon legends to scan the next Ring location. Each beacon can only be used once per match. Knowing the locations of all survey beacons on each map allows Recon legends to provide their squad with advance Ring information, enabling better rotation planning. Survey Beacon locations are fixed but reset each match.

Gravity Cannons are environmental traversal tools introduced on Storm Point. Players who enter a Gravity Cannon are launched in a fixed arc across a long distance, similar to the drop from the start of a match. Gravity Cannons allow rapid repositioning and are often used during rotations or when escaping unfavourable fights.
